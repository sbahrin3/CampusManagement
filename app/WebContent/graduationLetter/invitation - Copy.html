Dear Graduand

Invitation to the Cyberjaya University College of Medical Sciences 3rd Convocation Ceremony

On behalf of the University, we would like to congratulate on your success in completing your tertiary education and your great accomplishment. In celebrating your success, we cordially invite you to participate in the CUCMS 3rd Convocation Ceremony which will take place on Wednesday, 10 October 2012.

Further details of this event are as follows:

Venue: Putrajaya International Convention Centre (PICC)
Time: 8.00 a.m.

We wish to highlight important information regarding the Convocation Ceremony for your attention:

A. Collection of Regalia and Rehearsal

  1. The rehearsal is COMPULSARY to be attended by all graduands.
  2. Regalia can be collected on Tuesday, 9 October 2012 from 10.00 a.m. to 12.00 noon at Hall 1, Putrajaya International Convention Centre.
  3. The rehearsal will start at 2.30 p.m, Tuesday, 9 October, 2012
  4. Regalia must be returned between Thursday, 11 October 2012 until 10 November 2012 (for more information, please refer to the Convocation Guidelines and Procedure for Graduands Book).

B. Arrival

  1. The CUCMS 3rd Ceremony will take place in the Perdana Hall A, Putrajaya International Convention Centre PROMPTLY at 10.00 a.m., and will last approximately 2 hours. Doors will be open at 8.00 a.m. for guests and graduands. Graduands should plan their arrival NO LATER than 9.30 a.m. Ushers will be on hand to direct parents and guests to the event hall, where they will be seated.
  2. General parking are available at the basement of PICC.
